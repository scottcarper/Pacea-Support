## FAQ

### How do I start a routine?

Open a routine and tap **Start Routine**. The session will begin immediately and guide you through each step.

---

### Can I edit a routine after creating it?

Yes. Open the routine and tap **Edit Routine** to modify step titles, durations, or order.

---

### What happens if I skip a step?

Skipping a step moves you forward immediately. Only the time you actually spend in a session is counted toward elapsed time.

---

### What does “Elapsed Time” mean?

Elapsed Time reflects the **actual time spent** during a session.  
Skipped time is not included.

---

### Can I restart a step?

Yes. Tap **Restart Step** to begin the current step again from the beginning.

---

### Can I run a routine again after it finishes?

Yes. After a routine completes, tap **Run Again** to immediately start over.

---

### The timer is not updating on the Lock Screen or Dynamic Island

Make sure Live Activities are enabled:

iOS Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Live Activities

Also ensure the app is allowed to run in the background.

---

### Notifications are not appearing

Check:

iOS Settings → Notifications → Pacea → Allow Notifications

Also verify that Focus modes are not silencing alerts.

---

### How do I clear my history?

Go to the **History** tab and tap **Clear** in the top-right corner.

---

## Privacy

Pacea does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

- No accounts are required  
- No analytics or tracking are used  
- No data is transmitted off your device  

All routines and session history are stored locally on your device and never leave it.
